How to Write Effective Prompts for ASCII Art
To get the best results, your prompt should be descriptive and specific to the aesthetic of character-based art:
Specify Style: Use keywords like "ASCII art," "text art," "retro terminal," "cyberpunk ASCII," or "monospaced font".
Define Subject & Tone: Clearly describe the subject, setting, and mood (e.g., "a detailed skull," "a futuristic skyline," "high contrast," "dense characters").
Example Prompt: "Elegant ASCII art of a cat sitting on a windowsill, cyberpunk atmosphere, neon lights, high-detail ASCII texture effects". 
Tips for Best Results
High Contrast: Images with strong black-and-white contrast work best for conversion.
Resolution: Use higher resolution or "detailed" settings to ensure the characters form a clear image.
